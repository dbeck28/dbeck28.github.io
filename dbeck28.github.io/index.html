    • For complicated commercial transactions involving extraordinary sums of money, due diligence is necessary to complete sound business transactions. The primary goal of a due diligence investigation is to discover and analyze all of the material information necessary to enable the relevant parties to fully understand the business or the subject company and to identify and address any specific areas of concern.
  • - Surveillance
    • Surveillance is an investigative art that requires extensive knowledge of the target including their description, surroundings, activities, habits, schedules, associates and vehicle. Upon consultation and approval by the client, we use modern investigative technology and devices, formal surveillance techniques, as well as video and photographic equipment to ensure the target´s activities are expertly documented and reported. Our investigators use techniques including altering their personal appearance, changing vehicles and adapting to various ethnic environments in order to avoid detection and to provide a comprehensive surveillance package that achieves the client´s goals.
  • - Undercover Operations
    • Drawing from years of investigative experience with local and federal law enforcement agencies, we can analyze the needs of an undercover operation and determine what specific measures, tactics and personnel should be considered to bring the operation to a successful conclusion.

    Through client consultation and a sound understanding of the situation at hand, we can ensure the confidentiality of the investigation as well as compliance with state and federal guidelines. We provide clients with an accurate and detailed report for use in civil litigation, prosecutorial purposes or for other reasons as requested.

    Online Data Retrieval
    We maintain strong business relationships with numerous online data providers and information services that allow for instant searches of public records and other data sources for individuals and businesses. We discreetly research information online and can make in-person visits to various worldwide locations in order to obtain additional resources. All inquiries are made without revealing the subject, purpose or source of the inquiry. Note, however, that criminal records required for employment are researched through local law enforcement agencies and most require an authorization signed by the subject of inquiry.
  • Service of Process
    • Successful service of process often requires the investigator to conduct research, including knowledge of the subject´s activities and habits in order to complete the task. We know that in many instances, to avoid being served, subjects of a process service may attempt to hide while soliciting the help of friends and family members.

    Our investigators can make late night or early morning attempts, since the subjects are often not available during daytime hours. For especially complex cases, several investigators can be employed to ensure success of the assignment.
  • Skip Trace (Find a person)
    • Provided with a person’s first name and date of birth we can compile a list of all people in the United States who match that information, as well as each person´s last name and city of residence. We offer this service confident of success, but if we cannot provide at least one name with the information provided, we will not charge any fee. In addition, common names such as “John” can lead to thousands of results.

    If our search returns more than 50 names, we contact the client and obtain permission to continue with an added fee of $2.00 per name.

    The fee for this particular search is: $150
